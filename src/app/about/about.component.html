About Bird's Eye View

Bird’s Eye View is an interactive web app created by Jonathan Joly, Rob Schlenker and Julie Smyth. The next time you are craving an outdoor activity, look no further than Bird’s Eye View.

With access to over 70,000 hiking trails in the US, search by location to find the perfect hike near you. Each trail provided by Bird’s Eye View gives you hike difficulty, length and even the current weather conditions! But the fun doesn’t stop once you get to your trail, we give you up-to-the-minute reports on birds recently seen in that area. With thousands of images and access to over 10,000 sounds, you can identify the birds you see and mark them off as you spot them. Can you find them all?
